Services for Landlords

HOW DOES IT WORK?

We act on your behalf as your managing agents. We guarantee you a fixed rental income, find tenants, undertake the necessary checks and references. We will manage the property using our specialists for the term of the tenancy, with regular property inspections to ensure your asset is well maintained.

OTHER BENEFITS OFFERED TO LANDLORDS:

  • Your monthly rental figure guaranteed

  • Term to suit, normally 2+ years

  • Routinely tenanted in 2 to 3 weeks

  • All utility costs included

  • All routine maintenance included

  • Property maintained to industry quality standards
